# VISION

PulsePoll is a lightweight, temporary survey MVP.

The product is intentionally small:

- surveys are short-lived
- voting and results windows are limited
- product rules are explicit and documented
- backend behavior is simple and directly verifiable
- frontend integration happens after backend contract stability

The project should stay documentation-driven and verification-first.

The MVP is not trying to become a broad polling platform immediately. It should first prove that temporary surveys, voting rules, results visibility, consent-aware guest voting, and basic safety flows can work reliably with a small operational footprint.
